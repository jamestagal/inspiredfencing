Driveway double gates

Transforming a Holiday Home in Rye

Back in October 2018 we tackled a unique job in Rye. A Holiday home on a corner block was in desperate need of a new side fence and driveway gates for yard access, as well as a balustrade around an existing retaining wall in the yard. The completed fence was 12 metres long using standard palings at 1.65 M height with double plinth to compensate for the slope in the land. The double driveway gates had a steel frame and were finished in Colorbond Woodland Grey with a letterbox insert to match. For the balustrade we used treated pine plinth and posts for the most cost effective outcome for our client.

Features of double gates:

  • Easy access your private space
  • Contemporary design
  • Sturdy steel frame
  • Australian made Colorbond
